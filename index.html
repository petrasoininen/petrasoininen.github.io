About me

I’m Petra, a detail-oriented and empathetic UX/UI designer passionate about creating user-centered, clear, and visually appealing digital solutions. With a background in customer service, I have developed a strong ability to understand user needs and solve challenges efficiently. Driven by curiosity, continuous learning, and the desire to create meaningful experiences, I aim to design solutions that make people’s everyday lives easier and more enjoyable.

In addition to UX/UI design, I’ve studied digital marketing, project management, and Lean methodology, which have given me a holistic understanding of user-centered design, efficient workflows, and business-oriented service development.

I value smooth collaboration, but I’m also highly self-driven and organized, ensuring that projects progress efficiently from idea to execution. My goal is to craft designs that not only look beautiful but also bring real value to users.

I’m looking for opportunities to grow, work on meaningful projects, and create better user experiences. If you need an organized and motivated designer on your team, let’s connect!
