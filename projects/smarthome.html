Overview

Timeline

Aug 2023 - Dec 2023

Team

First, I worked with designers Maila Halme and Valtteri Otranen during the research phase of the project. After that, I worked with designers Ville Forsberg, Sofia Mesterton, and Roosa Väisänen on the remaining phases of the project

My role

The user research and overall design process were carried out collaboratively as a team. I was primarily responsible for the views related to home data tracking and how to manage all home functions at once.

This project was part of my studies at the Design Institute of LAB University of Applied Sciences.

Details of the project are covered by an NDA.

Goal of the project

Our goal was to research how potential customers perceive smart homes and their features, as well as what kinds of functions they would like a smart home to offer and what data they would be interested in tracking about their home. Based on the research, we created an interactive prototype for the client — a modern living application that combines digital home management with features such as viewing the community bulletin board.

Process

The process was divided into two parts — research and design — as the project took place over two courses.

During the research phase, my team focused on home management and its features. We were also assigned to compile the main survey, which included questions gathered from all teams involved in the project. Based on the research, we created user personas, user stories, and mapped out user journeys for the application.

In the design phase, my team concentrated on home management and data tracking. We also designed the prototype’s homepage and its key features, which were mandatory for all teams. The project details are covered by an NDA.

Final product

The home page provides quick and easy access to the home’s essential functions, ensuring smooth and efficient control.

The features of your home can be easily managed either as a whole or room by room. In addition, you can choose from preset themes that automatically adjust lighting and other settings to match the selected theme.

Home data such as temperature, humidity, and air quality can be easily monitored for the entire home or for individual rooms, and compared to the average of the housing company.

Key takeaways

In the project, we had the opportunity to work with a client who already had a strong brand identity, design system, and a well-defined project workflow. It was also interesting to continue the project with different teams after the research phase, as this allowed me to learn how to interpret research conducted by others and make design decisions based on their findings.
